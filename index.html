ABOUT US

Cabinets

In the beginning, JM Cabinets, Inc. was a sole proprietorship known as Jerry Morrison Cabinets in Oakland Park, Fl. In the late 70's and early 80's I began my woodworking career doing rough framing, decks, fences, handyman type work and finish carpentry. In the mid 80's I decided to concentrate on the cabinet and countertop trade.
